Nigeria

Nigeria is a country located in West Africa, bordered by Benin, Niger, Chad, and Cameroon, with a southern coastline along the Atlantic Ocean. It is the most populous country in Africa, with over 200 million people, and is known for its diverse cultures, languages, and ethnic groups. The country operates as a federal republic and its capital is Abuja, while Lagos remains the largest and most economically vibrant city.
