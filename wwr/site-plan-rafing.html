Overview

Purpose

The Rafing website is a platform for users to create and share their own rafing plans. Users can create a rafing plan by selecting a location, date, and time for their rafing event. Users can also view other users' rafing plans and join them. The website will also have a forum where users can discuss rafing-related topics.

Audience

The target audience for the Rafing website is outdoor enthusiasts who enjoy rafing and want to connect with other rafing enthusiasts. The website is also targeted at people who are new to rafing and want to learn more about the sport.

Color Palette

The website will use a color scheme of blue and white to reflect the water and sky, which are common elements in rafing. The font will be a clean and modern sans-serif font to give the website a contemporary look.
